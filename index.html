A Legacy of Love & Innovation

Born from a romance between Count Ferenc Nádasdy and Countess Ilona Zichy, this Tudor-style marvel was a masterpiece of its time. Completed in 1876, it boasted then-futuristic technology like central heating and a telephone system. After its revival through the National Castle Program, the castle now stands as a testament to both its romantic soul and its innovative spirit.
