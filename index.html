Nádasdy Castle

A Timeless Experience, Reimagined

A Legacy of Love & Innovation

Born from a romance between Count Ferenc Nádasdy and Countess Ilona Zichy, this Tudor-style marvel was a masterpiece of its time. Completed in 1876, it boasted then-futuristic technology. After its revival, the castle now stands as a testament to both its romantic soul and its innovative spirit.

The Experience

Forget passive observation. Our exhibition, "The Legend of the Castle, Revived," puts you at the heart of the narrative.

  • Digital Storytelling: Unlock augmented reality layers and untold stories with your personal tablet guide.
  • Ancestors Awakened: Witness the Nádasdy family portraits come alive in the Hall of Ancestors, sharing their tales through digital magic.
  • History in Your Hands: Create your own memories by trying on period costumes or sending a message on a vintage telegraph.
